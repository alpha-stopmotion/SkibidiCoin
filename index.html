What is SkibidiCoin?

SkibidiCoin is a memecoin on the binance smart chain network derived from the Internet meme: "skibidi toilet". The token was launched on the 26th of April 2024 with an initial supply of 100,000,000 tokens. Currently at time of writing, SkibidiCoin is worth approximately 1.2 USD.
